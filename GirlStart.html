Girlstart

Website redesign for non-profit organization

Problem

Girlstart is a non-profit organization intended to provide STEM education for young women lacking a clear design path and convoluted volunteer sign up process.

Solution

Create a more navigable and accessible interface to meet company goals and priorities.

Impact

The purpose of this redesign it to increase user accessibility and volunteer sign-ups.
